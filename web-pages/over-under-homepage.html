– Our Coffee –

[ Roasted by assembly ]

Assembly’s Head Roaster Nick met Beatriz in November of 2018 and was immediately impressed with the quality of her coffee production - constantly scoring 86+ on blind cuppings over the course of two weeks in Colombia.

Beatriz has been involved with the Red Associations project since its inception in 2017, of which Assembly is a founding member. This relationship is a perfect example of how small improvements in production and processing methodology can open up rewarding market opportunities for single producers in Colombia, outside of the already extremely impactful purchasing mechanism the Red Associations are becoming renowned for.
